About MS Memorial Netralaya

Our Story

Welcome to MS Memorial Netralaya, a trusted destination for comprehensive eyecare services. Established in 2008, our centre is located in the thriving small town of Dildarnagar in eastern Uttar Pradesh.

Our core mission is to serve the rural population that has little access to specialised eyecare. Led by Dr. Madhav Mukund, an ophthalmologist par excellence, our team is committed to improving vision and overall eye health for our patients.

Dr. Madhav Mukund earned his MBBS at the King George Medical College (KGMC) in Lucknow and his MS at the Ganesh Shankar Vidyarthi Memorial Medical (GSVM) College in Kanpur. With a wealth of knowledge, experience, and a well-earned reputation for delivering top-notch care and producing remarkable results, Dr. Madhav Mukund resolutely leads our team to improve the lives of our patients.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class eye care services to rural communities, ensuring that everyone, regardless of their location, has access to the best eyecare available. We bring state-of-the-art technology and contemporary facilities right to your doorstep.

Our Vision

To be the leading eye care provider in rural India, recognized for excellence in patient care, advanced technology, and compassionate service. We envision a future where no one suffers from preventable blindness due to lack of access to quality eye care.
