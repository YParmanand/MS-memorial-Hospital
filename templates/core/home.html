About MS Memorial Netralaya

Trusted Eye Care Since 2008

Welcome to MS Memorial Netralaya, a trusted destination for comprehensive eyecare services. Established in 2008, our centre is located in the thriving small town of Dildarnagar in eastern Uttar Pradesh. Our core mission is to serve the rural population that has little access to specialised eyecare.

At MS Memorial Netralaya, we pride ourselves on our unwavering commitment to addressing the unique challenges faced by rural communities when it comes to accessing top-notch specialised eyecare. That's why we bring state-of-the-art technology, sophisticated tools, and contemporary facilities right to your doorstep to deliver you the best eyecare services.
